SUPPORTING CHILDREN FOR A BRIGHTER FUTURE

Chubbies Foundation considers children facing the harsh realities of life, Through our Sponsorship Program the goal is to provide care and assistance to those children allowing them to thrive and receive an education. Children are the most vulnerable members of society who need consistent and comprehensive care and support to grow and prosper.

Scholarships can create an opportunity for children to go to school, make new friends, and become educated. Getting an education is a permanent solution to extreme poverty particularly the kind these kids face every day. The scholarship ensures that children are able to access the same level of schooling as those whose families can afford to.

EACH SCHOLARSHIP PROVIDES A CHILD WITH


School Fees

The school’s fees are paid in full giving the child the opportunity to go to school, learn knowledge, make friends and be themselves again.

Textbooks & Stationery

A backpack is provided, containing notebook, pens, pencils, colouring pens, eraser, pencil sharpener, geometry set, and textbooks

School Uniforms

The child is given full uniform, including a jumper, two shirts, pair of trousers, and two pairs of sturdy shoes, to get them through the year.

GET INVOLVED – SPONSOR A CHILD

Join our elite group of sponsors by providing a full scholarship to a child. This is a group of people that understand that as well as an education and financial support, they’re giving a child hope and a pathway to a brighter future, while sending them a crucial message – that they matter.

Sponsoring children for many sponsors is an immensely satisfying experience particularly where they witness the child’s growth and development in maturity over the course of the progress reports received. You can achieve the same by sponsoring a child’s scholarship.
